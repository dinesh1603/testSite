"FROM TIME IMMEMORIAL, THE HISTORY OF CIVILIZATION AGAIN AND AGAIN WARNS THE HUMAN RACE OF ITS MORBID MORALITY IN A CLARION VOICE. BUT, MANKIND IS LUDICROUSLY LURING ON WHAT IT HAS BEEN ALL THE WAY. ON THE LAST DAY OF EVERY YEAR, ALL THE NEWSPAPERS OF THE WORLD ASSESS THE EVENTS OF THE WHOLE YEAR AS VIOLENCE, BLEEDING, BRUISED, BATTERED, EXHILARATING OF TERRORISM, ETC. INDIA IS IN NO WAY EXCEPTIONAL FROM THIS."

THE INDIAN NEWS tidings say that any hope of peace or prosperity in this land appears Utopian. What a pathetic avalanche that mutilates the very standard of human nature...!

Increasing failure in streamlining the law and order, put a big question mark on the effectiveness of most of the State Governments in India. The political leaders' appeal for a new social order habitually goes unheeded, at least two murders per day, a rape on every third day and kidnapping every alternate day miserably fail the Government machinery to move forward. The recent terrorist problem also threatens the very core of the smooth functioning of every government department. It left the common mass to resign to their so-called fate in the absence of any assistance from the powers that be.

In the educational sector vandalism rules the roost. Still, some States continue to linger at the lowest rung of academic development. To push up the bottom line of illiteracy, our governments have introduced many unusual schemes to educate people at the grassroots level. For example, the Government of Bihar went to the extent of opening 'Charwaha Vidhyalaya' to educate the children of cattle grazers in their surroundings...!

Disruption in every development work coupled with the inadequate mobilisation of internal resources, however, belied all hopes of steering our land out of the cruel financial crisis. If we consider the number of currency notes alone is the criteria for the growth in economic front, then, ours is the number one in the world...!

The apathy of Government machinery made various military groups run parallel government mercilessly in some places. the police crackdown has neither whittled down the influence of these groups in their strongholds nor dented their determination to go ahead with their brutal activities by dispensing wild justice and passing even death sentences on those who are found guilty in their sights at the poverty-ridden and parched terrain.

In the mediatorially, cinema and lottery trades are gaining unprecedented momentum and the people go crazy after money and sex rather than anything else...!

Let us stop here and turn to the Scripture ... ... ...
