O! Doll! Beautiful doll!
You are my Doll!

I am a Doll
For my mum and dad!

You are a precious gift for me:
I am the one for my mum and dad!

You sing and dance for me!
I sing and praise my God above!

For, you are made by man: but,
I am made by God!
- Jenna, U.

[This Poem was written by this girl while she was eight years old.
Now she got married and blessed with a boy child.
She and her husband are working in an IT company.
Please remember the family of this girl in your prayers.]
